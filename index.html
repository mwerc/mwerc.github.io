Turning big ideas into real products.

We select roughly 15 startups and early-stage companies a year to join our 3-month, immersive, mentor-driven program. We are looking for teams that share our passion for making things in the following areas:

Energy

Drones

AR/VR

I-O-T

Robotics

3d Printing


We're looking for teams of engineers, scientists, and programmers across all funding stages. We will provide you access to unique production capabilities including high performance computing, rapid prototyping facilities and small scale production and testing equipment (for those potentially explosive startups!).

WERCBench Labs brings together world-leading companies that share a passion for making things.

Together with our corporate members, we provide access to technology and market insights that help you build your product and scale your business.

Customer & Sales Development

There is nothing we enjoy more than the thrill of the hunt! We're a passionate group of entrepreneurs, engineers and early-stage investors helping build tomorrow's innovations today. We devote considerable time to help our companies succeed.

We help companies build-measure-learn their way to success by getting them out of the building meeting with customers on a weekly basis and decreasing their time back to market once customer feedback has been collected. We support companies as they navigate their growth with support in team building, storytelling, PR, customer acquisition and marketing, business modeling, design, legal and more!

FAQ

You will receive a $20,000 grant; $10,000 at the start of the program and $10,000 upon completion of the program. You also have access to great perks such as [free hosting and services, sponsored meals, office space with the other teams, and more]. 2. What sorts of companies do you fund?

We fund technology companies in the [fields of energy, ar/vr, robotics, drones, [insert rest of categories]. We’re also looking for companies that can have national or worldwide reach. Your company must have a core team of engineers, scientists, or programmers to be considered. We are interested in builders who want to develop their technology into a product and business.>Learn more.

3. Do you fund companies that are competitive with each other?

Not in the same class/program, but sometimes otherwise, yes.

4. Will WERCBench take any ownership of my business?

At WERCBench, we are very focused on providing an on-ramp to entrepreneurship for builders. We strive to always make our environment as friendly as possible to the entrepreneur. WERCBench will never ask for ownership in your business.

5. Is my company “too far along” to apply to WERCBench?

Probably not. We’ve mentored companies with fully developed products and we’ve helped them with business models, introductions, financing, and more. Furthermore, nothing is too early. We are also accepting companies with no more than an idea and a dream, as long as they have a team of builders at the core.
