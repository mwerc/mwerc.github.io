FAQ

1. How much funding do WERCBench Labs companies receive?

You will receive a $20,000 grant; $10,000 at the start of the program and $10,000 upon completion of the program. You also have access to great perks such as... . There is immeasurable value in the connections and advice that you’ll receive when you start your company and develop your technology with WERCBench.

2. What sorts of companies do you fund?

We are interested in startups and early-stage companies in the fields of energy, ar/vr, robotics, drones, internet-of-things, and 3d printing. We’re also looking for companies that can have national or worldwide reach. Your company must have a core team of engineers, scientists, or programmers to be considered. We are interested in builders who want to develop their technology into a product and business.

3. Do you fund companies that are competitive with each other?

Not in the same class/program, but sometimes otherwise, yes.

4. Will WERCBench take any ownership of my business?

At WERCBench, we are very focused on providing an on-ramp to entrepreneurship for builders. We strive to always make our environment as friendly as possible to the entrepreneur. WERCBench will never ask for ownership in your business.

5. Is my company “too far along” to apply to WERCBench?

Probably not. We’ve mentored companies with fully developed products and we’ve helped them with business models, introductions, financing, and more. Furthermore, nothing is too early. We are also accepting companies with no more than an idea and a dream, as long as they have a team of builders at the core.

6. After I apply, I’m thinking of emailing many of the mentors to get them interested in what I’m doing. Good idea?

No. We receive many applications for each class, which is why we have an application process. Systematically emailing some or all of our mentors in order to try to influence them before being accepted into the program will hurt you more than it will help you. The mentors are dedicated to helping the companies that we select, but are not a part of the selection process.

7. Can I stop by your office to say hello?

Please don’t stop by our offices without an appointment. This is not a good example of persistence on your part and it will negatively influence your application. We are happy to consider taking an appointment, so please contact us instead.

8. Do I have to be there for the entire 3 month period?

No. It’s your company so you can come and go as you see fit. But we do require that you be primarily based on site during the program. This benefits you because you can take advantage of the numerous learning opportunities, networking and coaching that will be provided. WERCBench is a major commitment, and we expect you to be around most of the time.

9. Do I have to stay in Milwaukee when the program ends after 3 months?

Nope. We recognize that you are making the journey to Milwaukee for a short time. We want to provide you with the necessary tools and information for you to be successful no matter where you live.

10. Can I apply if our company or founders are not from the USA? Can you help with Visa/immigration issues?

Yes. Anyone from anywhere can apply. You do have to be legally in the U.S. during the program, of course. Once accepted, we can help with legal/immigration issues by leveraging our contacts and network to help. However, we cannot help with them in advance of acceptance.

11. What else does WERCBench provide?

We provide access to... .

12. I’m a single founder. Can I get accepted into WERCBench, or do you only look for teams?

While we don’t screen applications just because they have a single founder, it does make things more difficult. Because we are very focused on building things, we prefer that you have a well-rounded team of builders - software, hardware, etc.

13. Where will we live while we’re there?

That’s up to you – we don’t provide a place to live but can suggest resources for you.

14. How do you choose which companies to accept into the program and to fund?

WERCBench has a selection committee comprised of entrepreneurs, investors, subject matter experts, and industry professionals. We do like to see and work with great founders who are proposing startups that solve real problems or create meaningful innovations. For us, it’s about the team and your work ethic. The best things you can do to strengthen your application is: (i) round out your team with a the skills needed to build your idea; (ii) make progress on your prototype or product and reference it in the application; and (iii) show us you’ve really thought about your business and have done something about it.

15. Is my age a factor in your selection process?

No.

16. When will I hear back about my application?

We try our best to notify applicants as soon as possible about their acceptance into the program.

17. When is the deadline to apply and what’s the overall timeline?

Every applicant must complete the WERCBench application. WERCBench accepts applications during open application periods only and runs two WERCBench cohorts per year. Please don't send us business plans when the application period is closed. There are limited spots in each WERCBench class and startups are accepted based on the quality and completeness of their application. Each team, if selected to do so, will be interviewed by our outside selection committee. We suggest completing the "How to Build a Startup" lecture series on Udacity.com to get a head start for the program.

18. What will a typical day be like for a WERCBench founder?

It’s your company, so you’ll be hard at work on your product. On a weekly basis, we’ll organize workshops and meetings with our mentors/advisors. We also expect many of the mentors to drop into the office at various times throughout the program. In general, you’ll be working on your startup each day, just like all of the other founders in the program. We have developed a lean business startup process based on Steve Blank’s seminal work. There will be milestones and deliverables to reach, and we’ll make sure you have the tools to successfully complete our program.

19. Can I apply if I’m not from the United States?

Yes. As long as you and your team can be in the USA legally for the duration of the three month program, please do. We have accepted overseas founders in the past and love getting applications from all over the globe.

20. I’m concerned that if I apply, you won’t protect my intellectual property. Should I be worried about this?

We’re certainly not in the business of stealing ideas, and we treat application data as confidential information. Each year, we see a lot of applications and many of them include similar ideas. For this and other practical reasons, please don’t ask us to sign an NDA. Our application does not ask for your “secret sauce” and there is no reason you have to disclose this to us if you’re not comfortable doing so. We hope that our reputation speaks for itself on this topic.

21. Do you ever offer internships to people interested in WERCBench?

Yes, just send us a message that you’re interested.
