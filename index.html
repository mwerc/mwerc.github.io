Research

Prototyping & Production

State-of-the-art facilities to design, build, test, and ship any size product. Work alongside corporate R&D divisions and leading Universities.

Acceleration

Validate your Business Model and Reach Product-Market-Fit

The EIC is built around the leading energy, power and control corporations in the Midwest Region of the United States that serve on our expanding Energy, Power and Control consortium, as mentors, investors, and your potential customers.
We work with companies to help create energy independence through innovative new solutions tied to energy related activities such as; generation, transmission, distribution, storage, automation, power conversion and energy efficiency / conservation.

EPiC Ventures selects and supports entrepreneurs in three areas:

Acceleration - Development - Capital
Our goal is to support your venture long-term. That is why we select companies on a bi-monthly basis and give you free access to our facilities for one year. The first step is to connect you to a mentor who will prepare you for raising your next round of funding, landing new or expanding current customer relationships, and who will serve as your voice of reason in touch situations.

We take the Lean Approach

EPiC's Managing Directors are resident experts trained by Steve Blank in the Customer Development Methodology. They will guide your through a customized program to help you reach more customers, validate your hypotheses and grow your business.

Work space that fits your needs and keeps distractions to a minimal.

The EIC is artfully thoughtout by our award winning design and architectual partner, Kahler Slater. A mix of completely open working space, founder pods, and private offices make getting work done efficient. Located in the same building is one of the largest energy, power and control prototyping and production developments in the Midwest. With access to this state of the art equipment you can build almost anything.

The world's most promising Energy, Power and Control Startups.

Capital

EPiC is an early-stage accelerator fund that supports companies with $25,000 of seed capital for around 5% equity if selected and up to $150,000 of equity capital post program. We work with leading angel and venture funds around the world to support your growth.

Mentorship

We expect you to act and perform like a true entrepreneur, our mentors surely expect the same. You will learn to take command of your company as your distrupt the energy, power and control industry. From raising funds to acquiring new customers, you will be a force to be reckoned with.

Product-Market-Fit

Ultimately, our job is to help you sell more and we strongly believe in the customer development methodology. Our Managing Directors are worldrenouned experts in helping companies of all sizes achieve product-market-fit.
